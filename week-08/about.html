About Me

Hello! I'm Jocelyn Wang, a multidisciplinary designer with a passion for blending visual aesthetics with functional design. Currently pursuing my Master in Design at the University of Southern California, I bring a unique perspective that combines Eastern and Western design philosophies.

My journey in design started with visual communication and has expanded to include interactive media, UX/UI design, and even makeup artistry – all forms of creative expression that allow me to connect with people in different ways.

I believe that great design should not only be visually appealing but also meaningful and accessible. Whether I'm working on a brand identity, an interactive installation, or creating a makeup look, my goal is to evoke emotion and create memorable experiences.

  • Experimenting with new makeup techniques and products
  • Traveling to experience different cultures and aesthetics
  • Photography and visual storytelling
