BRAND IDENTITY 2024

DAI XIA JI Visual Design

Developed a full brand identity system for Dai Xia Ji, China’s leading shrimp paste brand. Designed a series of custom icons and branded applications including airline meal boxes and promotional coupons, blending traditional cultural motifs with contemporary visual aesthetics.

FOOTWARE DESIGN 2025

Playful Platform Sneakers Design

Created a pair of chunky, playful platform sneakers that explore the theme of customization and modular fashion. The design emphasizes vibrant color composition, geometric rhythm, and tactile material contrasts, allowing wearers to express individuality through pattern and form.

MAKEUP ARTISTRY 2023–Present

Jolihub Makeup Studio

Founded and operated a professional makeup studio since 2023. As a senior makeup artist from China, I specialize in natural, refined, and emotionally resonant looks, inspired by cinematic portraiture and modern aesthetics. The studio’s identity highlights authenticity, softness, and artistic confidence.

INTERACTIVE MEDIA 2024

PÁSION

An interactive narrative game addressing the theme of cyberbullying. Through a 2.5D surreal visual style, the project immerses players in the emotional journey of a character suffering from online violence, combining story-driven interaction, symbolic space design, and audiovisual tension to evoke empathy.

UX/UI & SERVICE DESIGN 2024

JoliHub — Cosmetic Sales & Service Platform

Designed a digital platform that connects makeup artists with cosmetic brands, enabling artists to recommend and sell products directly through their beauty services. By integrating user research, service blueprint, and information architecture, the system reduces flop purchases and enhances customer experience through personalized recommendations.

INTERACTIVE DESIGN 2024

FRACTURED ATLAS

An experiment-based interactive installation exploring how digital distractions affect human perception in natural environments. Using point cloud simulation and real-time behavioral recognition, the project visualizes participants’ perceptual field changes when exposed to external stimuli such as phone notifications or sound cues.
